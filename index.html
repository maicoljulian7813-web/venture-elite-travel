Introduction

Welcome to Finland

Finland is a country in Northern Europe. It has about 5.6 million people. One famous place is Rovaniemi, with about 65,000 residents. Finland is a clean, modern, and safe country. Finnish people love nature, and they respect silence and order.


Finland is famous for winter sports. People play ice hockey, and many people go skiing and go ice skating in winter.

  • There are forests and lakes.
  • There are reindeer.
  • There are igloos.
  • There are museums.
  • There are ice hotels.

Sports & Activities

What Can You Do?

⛷Tourists can visit Santa Claus Village and see the Aurora Borealis at night.
🏂They can go snowboarding, can go skiing, and can go ice skating. They can also ride sleighs and go snowmobiling.
⌚Tourists could use a smartwatch to check their activity and GPS route. They use their eyes to watch the sky. They should sit quietly to enjoy the Northern Lights.

⚠ Rules to Remember


  • Tourists should move carefully and act safely.
  • They can't throw garbage in nature.
  • They can't disturb wild animals.

Eco-Friendly Tips

Be Kind to Nature 🌿

  • Tourists should carry a reusable bottle — the tap water is very clean.
  • They could use the local bus to reduce pollution.
  • They should respect wild animals and forests.
  • They could walk in the city or use electric transport.
  • They can't throw garbage in natural places.

👗 What to Wear

Tourists should wear warm clothes and waterproof clothes because the weather is very cold in winter.

Finland FAQ ❓

When do the Northern Lights appear?

Usually between 9:00 p.m. and 2:00 a.m.

Can I pay with a contactless card?

Introduction

Welcome to Egypt

Egypt is a country in North Africa. Its capital city is Cairo, with about 22 million residents. Egypt is famous for history, culture, and art. The people are friendly, and the Nile River is very important in the city.


Egypt is also famous for soccer. Many people play soccer, and it is a very popular sport in the country.

  • There are pyramids.
  • There are camels.
  • There are mosques.
  • There are markets.
  • There are amazing sunsets.
🇪🇬 Egypt / Cairo

Sports & Activities

What Can You Do?

🏛Tourists can visit the pyramids, explore the desert, ride a camel, and can also go sandboarding.
📱They could use a fitness tracker to check their physical effort. They could use a sports camera for activities.
🦵They use their legs and feet to keep balance. They use their arms to control movement.

⚠ Rules to Remember

  • They can't take pictures without permission.
  • They can't mistreat animals.
  • They should act respectfully in cultural places.
